Zeinab Abboud

I'm a PhD candidate in Computer Engineering at the MedicalLab in Polytechnique Montreal - University of Montreal and at Mila. I am also currently the Program Manager of the Responsible AI Leadership program by AI 2030 by FinTech4Good

Prior to my PhD I worked as a Metrology Engineer for 4+ years at illumina, and Teledyne DALSA Semiconductor, developing and deploying metrology methods for manufacturing quality control. At illumina, I led the algorithm for automated defect detection, software development, and full lifecycle integration for quality control of new product introduction (NPI). I hold an M.A.Sc in Engineering Physics from Polytechnique Montreal - University of Montreal and a BSc. in Nanoscience with a double minor in Mathematics and Physics from University of Guelph.
